Mastering the Soundscape
The core innovation of HereOne lies in its Real-Time Audio Filtering engine. Unlike standard 
headphones that isolate the user, HereOne allows for the selective transparency of sound. 
Through an intuitive mobile interface, users can "EQ the world," muting the roar of a jet engine 
while crystalizing the voice of a person sitting directly across from them.
